I'm a final year Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering student at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology. During my under-graduation, I completed multiple research projects under the guidance of Dr. Himani Bansal and Dr. Dinesh C. S. Bisht broadly in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Currently, I’m a Research Intern at Networked Wireless Systems Lab, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT Hyderabad), working with Dr. Antony Franklin (Associate Professor) on the Learning-Based 360 video caching with Mobile Edge Computing. I work on implementing the Asynchronous Advantage Actor-Critic (A3C) algorithm of Reinforcement Learning and Long short-term memory (LSTM) network along with Convolutional Neural Network(CNN) for caching of videos and classification of tiles in user's Field of View(FoV).

I am interested in solving real-world problems using machine learning and designing new interventions for societal impact, especially in Healthcare, Cognitive Science, and Robotics. I am still exploring the space of problems in these areas and yet to decide on any specific problem on which I would like to do my research.
